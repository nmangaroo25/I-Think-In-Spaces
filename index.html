About Us

I Think In Spaces, LLC is a New York City and New York State–certified MBE architectural design and drafting firm providing dependable pre-construction and documentation support for architectural, engineering, and construction teams.

The firm supports project teams through the preparation of clear floor plans, coordinated layouts, and well-organized drawing sets that align with federal, state, and local requirements.

Led by a principal with professional experience supporting multi-disciplinary design teams, I Think In Spaces utilizes advanced CAD and BIM workflows to assist prime consultants and subcontractors with drafting coordination, model support, and constructability documentation to help keep projects organized and aligned.
