About Us

I Think In Spaces is comprised of drafters and designers who specialize in creating detailed layouts, plans, and illustrations of buildings, structures, systems, and components based on engineering and architectural specifications.

Utilizing the latest computer-aided drawing and Building Information Modeling (BIM) software, we bring accuracy, efficiency, and clarity to every project.

We also partner with real estate agents and contractors. Realtors often need architects and designers for buyers who want to renovate, and contractors frequently prefer to include a designer on their bid team. By offering collaborative packages, we help clients visualize potential renovations — giving our partners an edge to win more deals.

Whether supporting residential, commercial, or specialty projects, our mission is to deliver drawings that inspire confidence, support collaboration, and set the foundation for successful outcomes.
