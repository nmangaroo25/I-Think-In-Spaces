About Us

I Think In Spaces, LLC is an MBE-certified firm providing dependable pre-construction drafting and design support for architectural, engineering, and construction teams. We prepare clear floor plans, coordinated layouts, and organized documentation that follow federal, state, and local requirements.

Using advanced BIM tools, we help project teams work efficiently and maintain consistent quality. During the construction phase, we also support subcontractors with BIM shop drawing coordination and clash detection to keep field teams aligned and minimize conflicts.

Our services support architects, engineers, contractors, and real estate professionals across government, private, and specialty projects. From facility upgrades to new construction, we focus on accuracy, clarity, and steady collaboration so your team can move forward with confidence.

What We Provide

  • Precise drafting and BIM support integrated into your workflow
  • Code-aligned documentation ready for review by licensed professionals
  • Reliable production that supports smooth coordination
  • Flexible assistance for diverse project types
